Most on student visas

Most of them are in the U.S. on an F-1 student visa for full-time academic study. Others have J-1 exchange visitor visa status or H-1B specialty worker status. H-1B visa holders have a bachelor's degree or equivalent and are typically employed by the university in their area of study.
